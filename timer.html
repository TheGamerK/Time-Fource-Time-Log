Welcome to Time Fource Time Log

Here you can record your time and record what you did fro that time.



HOW IT WORKS.
1) Hit the start button. Time will start to roll and you can start working.
2) Once you have completed a task you can hit the "LOG TIME" button and a popup will apper, type anything you want like what work you did. Once you have clicked "OK" it will go into a log and keep track of what you have done.
3) When you are finished for the day you can hit the "DOWNLOAD LOG" button and it will take you to another page and show you what you logged on the last page.
4) when your ready hit the "DOWNLOAD" button and a Log.txt will be downloaded and you will have a log of your time.
